Speech to Text

Ancroo Workflow Demo — record audio, transcribe with Whisper

🎤

Press Alt+Shift+R or use the Ancroo side panel to start recording. The audio is sent to Whisper for transcription and the result is copied to your clipboard.

How to use: Open the Ancroo side panel, select "Speech to Text", and click Execute to start recording. Speak clearly, then stop the recording. The transcription appears in your clipboard, ready to paste.
